Bikeshare Station Networks VS Business Networks

There are currently 264 bikeshare docking stations and over 14,000 businesses in Philadelphia.

Bikeshare stations are mainly located in the city centre and surrounding neighbourhoods, reflecting that the bikesharing network mainly caters to short-distance trips in the city centre and nearby areas.

Businesses are more densely distributed throughout the city, covering a wider area.

Both the bikeshare and business networks have significant high-density clustering in the city centre, suggesting that the city centre is both a core of business activity and cycle trips.
