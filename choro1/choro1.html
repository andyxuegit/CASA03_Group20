Distribution of Bikeshare Stations with Socioeconomic Characteristics

Population density is higher in the outer area, especially the north of Philadelphia, than in the city centre. However, bikeshare stations are predominantly located in the city centre and to the west and south of the city centre. Not only the median annual household income but also the white population proportion are relatively higher in the city centre and south city. The area north to the city centre, with a low median household income, has a relatively sparse number of bikeshare stations. Despite the lower median income and higher number of bikeshare stations on the east side of the city, this area has two universities, which means that these users are mostly students instead of real disadvantaged people (Caspi, 2023).
